     Event     

It's all about fun. We gather, we reach out to each other, communication: The fundamental interaction that every person requires and is utterly inevitable. For the sake of leveraging it, more interaction is necessary. We learn from each other as a friend, as a wholehearted mentor.

  Organiser  

Information Technology Society, aka ITS, is a society in UTAR which joined by many and mainly Software Engineering students, had turned into Course-based from Interest-based last year. Our mission is to bring knowledge, joy and love to every each of our member and helping others.

       Date       

This event has been scheduled at 14th of June this year, which is Sunday of the week. It is week 3 into the May semester for fellow UTARians, so the time is perfectly loose for students to take part in the event.

       Time       

Participants may come over around 4 or 5pm that day, but the event begins at 6pm sharp, and we are having a 20 minutes loose period after the event start to wait for more guests to arrive. Committee are advised to come as early as 7am in the morning for preparation to kick-off. The ending time is estimated to be around 10pm.

Participants

The targeted participants for this event is students who are currently taking Software Engineering course in UTAR. So if you are studying as Y1, Y2 or Y3 at that semester, you can participate as well. We also have target alumnis which persue their job at IT industries after graduating, back here to gather together.

Don't worry if you are not a SE student, you are very welcomed to join us too! Our ideal participants for this event is 150+ people, so please feel free to help us realise this dream too!

   Location   

The event will be hosted at a luxury cafe in the C180 area. The hall is know as Black Tower. The full address is shown somewhere in the page. We had provided a google map coords for you to identify the location easier. Warning: It is very high class so viewer discretion is adviced.

     Theme     

Retro style is a style that is imitative or consciously derivative of lifestyles, trends, or art forms from the historical past, including in music, modes, fashions, or attitudes. It may also be known as "vintage inspired". We are excited to tell that retro and vintage style will be our main theme for this event, bringing us back to the 90's. The reason we came out this idea is the ever-changing pace of the world where evolutions outsource the speed in time.

     Attire     

Participants are suggested to wear smart casual clothes as our event is not a formal event. Ideally shirts with retro colors so that it is synchronous with our theme.
